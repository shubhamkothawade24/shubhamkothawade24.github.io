Vision of TEAM PHALANX

Team Phalanx racing has been a competitive racing team since 2016. It started with a aim of providing college students with practical knowledge. Over the years, the team has transformed and equipped with vast knowledge. The aim has now risen its bars, The team now believes in achieving excellence in the engineering field.

Team Phalanx Racing is an establishment of Government College Of Engineering And Research, Avasari. The team has become a learning center for all the Mechanical in addition to Automobile students in the college who love to research with hands-on experience. The team participates in different go-kart championships across India.

The team is backed by College Administration and experienced faculty. Phalanx racing is the only government college team from Maharashtra consequently. We have remarkable technical and non-technical team members.
